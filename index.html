About Virtus Advisors

Virtus Advisors is a Dallas-based commercial real estate consulting and technology firm. We work directly with property owners, developers, tenants, and asset managers on the ground-level work that drives projects forward — vendor management, contract negotiation, dispute resolution, and full-cycle project delivery.

We're not a brokerage. We're not a fund. We are operators who have managed ground-up commercial developments, taken over failing renovation projects mid-stream, and built technology to make CRE professionals more effective.
