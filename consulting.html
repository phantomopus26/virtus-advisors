What We Offer

Turnkey Project Management for Commercial Real Estate

Virtus Advisors provides hands-on commercial development and project management services. We step in where coordination fails, budgets slip, and timelines stall — and we get projects moving again.

Our principal has managed ground-up commercial developments and taken over mid-stream renovation projects that were off track. We're available as a fee consultant, an equity partner, or both depending on what the deal calls for.

We work lean, move fast, and communicate clearly. No bloated teams, no vague deliverables — just direct project leadership with real accountability.
